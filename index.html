↩️ Returns Policy

Easy, hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery.

Our Return Policy

We want you to be 100% happy with your order. If for any reason you're not, here's what to know:

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • Perishable items are non-returnable but we offer full replacements
  • Refund processed within 3-5 business days
  • Contact us via WhatsApp for fastest resolution

❓ FAQ

Answers to our most commonly asked questions.

Are all products certified organic?

Yes! Every product carries at least one of: USDA Organic, India Organic, or FSSAI certification.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery is 2–3 business days. Express delivery (same/next day) is available in select cities.

Do you offer wholesale / bulk orders?

Yes! Contact us via WhatsApp or email for wholesale pricing and corporate orders.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. Payments are processed through Razorpay with 256-bit SSL encryption and 3D Secure.
